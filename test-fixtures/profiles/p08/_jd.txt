**Senior Platform Engineer**
*Northwind Logistics — Seattle, WA (Hybrid: 3 days in office)*

---

**About Northwind Logistics**

Northwind Logistics is a Series D freight-tech company moving $2.3B of cargo annually across North America. Our platform orchestrates routing, dispatch, and real-time visibility for a fleet of 14,000+ carrier partners and 400 shipper customers including Costco, Sysco, and Dollar Tree. Our infrastructure runs 170+ microservices across two regions, processing 8M+ events per hour during peak. We're a 95-person engineering org that values shipping, deep technical ownership, and a no-blame postmortem culture. We've been remote-friendly since 2019 and have a track record of promoting senior engineers from within — four of our eight staff engineers started here as mid-levels.

---

**The Role**

We're hiring a Senior Platform Engineer to join our 11-person Core Platform team. You'll own significant chunks of our AWS infrastructure, our CI/CD pipelines, and the internal developer platform that ~80 product engineers use daily. This is a hands-on IC role (no direct reports, but strong influence across teams) reporting to the Director of Platform Engineering.

We've just kicked off a 14-month migration from ECS to EKS, and you'll be one of the two engineers leading that effort. Day-to-day you'll be writing Terraform, debugging GitHub Actions runners, designing IAM policies, and pairing with product teams who need help getting their services production-ready.

---

**What You'll Do**

- Design, build, and operate our multi-region AWS infrastructure (EKS, RDS, MSK, S3, CloudFront) serving 8M+ events/hour
- Lead the migration of ~60 services from ECS to EKS over the next 14 months alongside one other senior engineer
- Build and maintain our GitHub Actions-based CI/CD platform, currently supporting 170+ services with 1,400+ deploys/week
- Develop our internal developer platform (Backstage-based) including golden-path templates, service catalog, and deployment tooling
- Define and enforce platform standards: observability (Datadog), security baselines, SLI/SLO definitions, and cost guardrails
- Participate in an on-call rotation (1 week per quarter, with a $500 bonus per shift) for platform-level incidents
- Mentor mid-level engineers and conduct technical reviews for platform-facing PRs
- Write thorough runbooks, postmortems, and design docs — we value documentation that outlives the author

---

**Requirements**

- 6+ years of professional software engineering experience, with at least 3 years focused on platform, infrastructure, or SRE work
- Deep hands-on AWS expertise — you've operated production workloads at scale (we'd estimate 50+ services or equivalent complexity)
- Strong proficiency in at least one systems language: Go, Rust, or C/C++ (our platform tooling is primarily Go)
- Production Kubernetes experience — you understand networking (CNI, service mesh), operators, and you've debugged control-plane issues at 2am
- Significant Terraform experience (we use Terraform Cloud, ~180 modules) and a strong opinion about module boundaries
- Solid CI/CD chops: you've designed pipelines from scratch, not just used them — GitHub Actions, Buildkite, or similar
- Excellent written communication — async-first culture means your docs and ADRs carry real weight
- Bachelor's degree in CS or equivalent practical experience
