Clinical Care Coordinator — Inpatient Medicine
Full-Time | Days, No Weekends | Lakeview, OH (On-Site)

About Mercy Ridge Health Network
Mercy Ridge Health Network is a nonprofit, five-hospital system serving the Lake Erie region and surrounding counties, with more than 1,800 licensed beds, a Level II trauma center, and over 11,000 caregivers. Our Inpatient Medicine service line spans complex medical-surgical, step-down, and observation units, and we are nationally recognized for our heart failure, sepsis, and geriatric fracture programs. We are committed to equitable care, interdisciplinary teamwork, and using data to drive better outcomes for our patients and communities.

Position Summary
The Clinical Care Coordinator (CCC) is the central clinical operations lead for our 36-bed Inpatient Medicine unit at Mercy Ridge Lakeview Medical Center. Reporting to the Director of Case Management with a dotted-line partnership to the Unit Medical Director, the CCC partners with bedside RNs, hospitalists, social workers, pharmacists, and therapy teams to move patients safely and efficiently from admission through discharge. The ideal candidate is a seasoned RN who still loves the clinical side of nursing but is ready to step into a leadership role that shapes care plans, reduces readmissions, and coaches a frontline team.

Responsibilities
- Coordinate individualized, evidence-based care plans for a caseload of 18–22 medical-surgical patients with multi-system diagnoses, in collaboration with the attending hospitalist and interdisciplinary team.
- Conduct daily multidisciplinary rounds (MDR), driving agenda, documenting action items, and ensuring follow-through on discharge barriers such as home health, durable medical equipment, and post-acute placement.
- Serve as Epic superuser for the unit: build and maintain order sets, care pathways, and best-practice alerts; partner with IT and informatics to test and roll out enhancements.
- Review length-of-stay, readmission (30-day all-cause), and observed-to-expected mortality data weekly; identify trends and lead targeted improvement initiatives.
- Mentor and coach a team of 28 bedside RNs and two patient care assistants, including new-hire onboarding, competency validation, and real-time clinical escalation support.
- Act as primary liaison with case management, utilization review, and payer partners to align clinical care with medical necessity documentation and authorization requirements.
- Lead or co-lead one unit-based performance improvement project per quarter (e.g., reducing CAUTI, improving early mobility, transitioning patients off telemetry safely).
- Participate in shared governance, nursing council, and hospital-wide committees as a clinical representative for Inpatient Medicine.

Requirements
- Active, unrestricted Registered Nurse (RN) license in the State of Ohio (or compact state with Ohio endorsement within 60 days of start).
- Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) required; MSN or enrollment in an MSN program strongly preferred.
- Minimum 5 years of recent bedside nursing experience in an acute care medical-surgical, step-down, or complex acute care setting.
- Demonstrated experience coordinating care plans, facilitating multidisciplinary rounds, and managing complex discharges.
- Proficiency with Epic EHR, including build/optimization, reporting workbench, and Care Coordination functionality.
- Prior charge nurse, preceptor, or formal leadership experience with proven ability to coach and give feedback to peers.
- Strong working knowledge of CMS Conditions of Participation, InterQual/Milliman criteria, and value-based purchasing metrics.
- Excellent communication, prioritization, and conflict-resolution skills; comfortable having difficult conversations with physicians, patients, and families.

Nice to Have
- Certified Case Manager (CCM) or Accredited Case Manager (ACM) credential.
- Lean Six Sigma Yellow or Green Belt, or equivalent quality improvement training.
- Experience with remote patient monitoring, transitional care programs, or hospital-at-home models.
- Bilingual English/Spanish, given our regional patient population.

- Tuition reimbursement toward an MSN or DNP, plus certification pay for CCM/ACM.
